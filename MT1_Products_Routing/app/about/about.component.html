About Us

The number of persons shopping online has increased dramatically throughout the quarantine and lockout period. People all across the world regard online apparel as a depiction of current fashion trends within a culture. Antholem's Clothing (A.C.) is an online clothing business that provides bright, comfy men's and women's clothing at a reasonable price.

Mr. Sam Antholem Manalo formed the company on January 21, 2022, and it began operations in Mabalacat, Pampanga. This company strives to meet and surpass customers' expectations by offering high-quality, cheap pre-owned clothing that enhances their appearance and boosts their confidence.
